However, Ruqqie and transformative artistic works based on Ruqqie may not be sold, affixed to merchandise, or otherwise monetized without explicit written permission from Ruqqus LLC. * [Ruqqie](/assets/images/logo/ruqqie.png) ## Terminology Ruqqus is broken into numerous smaller messaging and commenting boards focused around a variety of topics. These boards are called "Guilds". Guild names always start with a `+` symbol, and are usually (though not always) named after their topic of interest. For example, [+news](/+news) is a Guild focused on sharing and discussion of news and current events. The users who create and moderate Guilds are called "Guildmasters". Guildmasters are regular users, and have no affiliation with Ruqqus LLC beyond having a ruqqus.com account. Users can submit posts to Guilds. They can also can add comments as replies to posts or other comments. Guildmasters cannot hide or remove content. They can "kick" off-topic posts out of their guild. Such content then appears in [+general](/+general), an unmoderated Guild that functions as a catch-all area. Similarly, Guildmasters can "yank" content from +general into their own guild. When a post is kicked or yanked, all of its comments move with it. When referring to the Ruqqus social media platform, use "Ruqqus" or "ruqqus.com". When referring to the business which owns and operates the Ruqqus platform, use "Ruqqus LLC". When referring to the owners, staff, or other elements of Ruqqus LLC, use "Ruqqus staff", "Ruqqus owners", or similar phrasing. Users of the Ruqqus platform may be informally called "Ruqqies", or formally "Ruqqus users". ## Coloring The name of the Ruqqus purple color is "porpl".
