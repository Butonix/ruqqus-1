{% endif %} {% filter markdown %} # Guild Siege In order to ensure that Ruqqus Guilds can be curated and managed, users may use this page to try to "lay siege" to, and take over, a guild with inactive Guildmasters. You may attempt to siege a guild once every 7 days. Any siege attempt - whether successful or not - will trigger this cool-down period. ## Requirements In order to successfully siege a guild you must meet the following requirements: * Have a 30 day old account, and * Wait 7 days since your last siege attempt, and * Not be exiled from the guild you are attempting to siege, and * Have Rep in the guild you are attempting to siege (exact requirement depends on guild size and age) Additionally, no current Guildmaster (or no Guildmaster above you, if you are a Guildmaster already) may have done any of the following in the past 60 days: * Create a post in the guild being sieged * Create a comment in the guild being sieged * Perform a mod action in the guild being sieged Guildmasters who are permanently banned, or who have deactivated their Ruqqus account, will always count as inactive, regardless of actual activity. Sieges against [+general](/+general), or admin-controlled Guilds such as [+Ruqqus](/+Ruqqus), [+RuqqusPress](/+RuqqusPress) or [+GuildSiege](/+GuildSiege) will always fail. Finally, you may not Siege a guild with the intention of vandalizing it or subverting its purpose.
